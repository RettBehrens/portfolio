HI, I'M RETT BEHRENS


I'm a full stack web developer based out of Denver, Colorado... but anywhere with wifi counts as an office. I enjoy taking ideas from "hey wouldn't it be cool if..." to "check this out!".

In addition to working as a developer, I'm also a teaching assistant and tutor with a coding bootcamp in the Denver market.

I stay up to date on the latest frameworks, languages, platforms, and technologies by regularly attending conferences, seminars, networking events, and fun meetups so I can learn from and be a resource to other developers in the field.

When I'm not coding, I'm probably soaking in the hot springs of southwestern Colorado, sipping local craft beer and planning the next days adventure.
